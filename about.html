About the TRAA

Who are we ?

For the enhancement, protection and promotion of a viable, multi-species fishery within the Thames River watershed.

TRAA main points 1

A volunteer organization, governed by a formal constitution and by-laws

TRAA main points 2

Formed in 1986 by anglers concerned with the state of the fishery in the Thames River watershed

TRAA main points 3

Group of active members supported by those who just want to be part of the solution

TRAA main points 4

We are “hands-on” environmental group who likes to work at the grassroot level

Our Mission

Protect

Protect, enhance and regenerate a viable, multi-species, year-round fishery.

Participate

Promote and participate in environmental clean-up, pollution control and rehabilitation.

Promote

Promote environmentally sound sport fishing practices.

Re-establish

Breed, relocate and stock to re-establish or augment appropriate species of fish.

Form

Form partnerships with community businesses.

Increase

Increase public awareness of the unique diversity of the Thames River Watershed.
