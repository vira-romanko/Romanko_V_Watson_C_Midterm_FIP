How memberships help

Your membership adds another voice to our efforts in protecting and enhancing aquatic habitats in the London & area community. The Thames River Angler's Association will always be here, putting money into projects that encourage the river habitat to flourish. Every bit counts.

What do you get?

Being a member of the TRAA has its perks. You get access to certain classes and involvements; like rod building! Don't forget, you are supporting a cause and becoming a part of the solution. Not to mention, you get a free TRAA sticker with your membership.

How to join

Interested in becoming a part of the TRAA? Send us a message! Our memberships are at a low cost of $25 per year. Still not sure? Come and get to know us at one of our upcoming meetings. Guests are always welcome!
